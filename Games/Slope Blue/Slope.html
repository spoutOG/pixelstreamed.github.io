Slope

Slope 3 Version 1

Slope is an exciting endless running game based on the slope series. Drive a ball rolling down a series of the slope, avoid obstacles and get a high score.

Your mission is to control a ball rolling on the slopes, avoid obstacles, and keep your eye on the road.

It would be best if you got the ball rolling as far as possible without crashing dead walls or falling into the deep space. The longer you run on the race, the faster the ball rolls on the course. The ball starts moving slowly and becomes faster as far as you can go. Your score will increase by 1 point after each successful jump that you perform. Try to control the ball for as long as possible because the course is endless with no level or stages. Gain your score to become the top admirable player on the leaderboard! However, be careful and pay attention to the ball and the infinite racetrack. There are many crazy moving obstacles, treacherous pits, roadblocks, and tricky objects on the slopes. Moreover, the ball also can respond in real-time with each slight adjustment. Therefore, you should not hold the keyboard keys for longer because the ball can fall into the deep.

Slope Game

Slope Game is an infinite series including various versions: Slope, Slope 2, Slope 3, and Slope Run. The most famous feature is the endless slopes.

When playing this running game, all players focus on controlling a ball. It's correct! It's not challenging to control your ball while this moving way has a great effect on your achievement. To better understand the importance of movement, let's move to the next section about Slope Game.

Importance of control

As you can know, this online game focuses on your control skills to gain high achievements. If you want to get a high score, you need to navigate your ball as far as possible and avoid any obstacles. In order to achieve these goals, control is the most important factor.

Specifically, thanks to controls, players can conquer the following challenges.

  • First, slopes are in various sizes and shapes, so your ball can’t roll in straight. Instead, the ball needs a guide to run different slopes safely. If not, it’s easy for your ball to fall into the Slope space.
  • Second, there are dangerous obstacles with different shapes and movements. All players have to avoid colliding with these obstacles, especially moving objects. This feature requires players to show a fast reaction and high control skills to overcome the second challenge.
  • Finally, to adapt to the speed of the Slope Game, control is indispensable. Your ball always rolls at a fast speed and this speed will increase following the time and distance. In this condition, control is the only way to conquer the speed challenge.

Through these 3 challenges, players can realize that control plays a significant role in gaining high victory.

Differences of Slope Game

This Slope version is different from other games thanks to its slopes and challenges. Because almost all of the slope editions have the same gameplay and control, it’s difficult to find differences. If you want to find differences, let’s see their slopes and challenges.

No slope is completely the same in different Slope versions. Slopes may have common characteristics in terms of size, shape, inclination, slope, and movement. However, these elements are different in each version to create new experiences. In particular, the challenges from obstacles are more diverse with many sizes, shapes, and movements. Every change makes the Slope Game unique.

Slope 3 challenges

When you first play this game, you can think that the gameplay is simple and easy to control. It is believed that all you need to do is use keyboard arrow keys to drive and dodge obstacles. That is true, but not all. Mastering the third slope version is more complicated than you thought. Why? The difficulty level of this game belongs to the high speed of the ball which requires exact reflexes and fast hand speed. Besides that, the slope obstacles are unpredictable and become harder after the race you overcome. These obstacles can move based on the changes in slopes and platforms. As a result, the challenges from slopes always is a control test for online players.

How to control

You can play with the arrow or A/D keys to avoid the obstacles on the slope.

  • A/Left arrow = Move left
  • D/Right arrow = Move right

Tips to get a high score

  • Keep the ball in the middle of the track
  • Plan your moves while the ball is rolling
  • Be careful and notice the appearance of obstacles
